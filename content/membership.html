Membership

By joining the Durham divers, you will meet new dive buddies, make new friends and have access to all the organized (and supervised) club dives throughout the season as well as our social events and fun!

A great way to meet enthusiastic divers and explore the underwater world together!

Membership costs** are as follows:

Single Member - $85/year
This is our normal membership price and accounts for the majority of all members in our club. Insurance while participating in club events included!

Additional Family Members - $55/year
The family membership provides membership to the club for all family members certified to dive that live at the same address. The fee covers the cost of their insurance while on club dives.

**Organized dives, club safety gear maintenance and improvement, charter deposits, club liability insurance, mailings, bank fees, admin.
