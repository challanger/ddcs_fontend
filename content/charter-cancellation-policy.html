Charter Cancellation Policy:

  • Dives cancelled within 30 days of the charter are eligible for a refund ONLY if all the spaces on the boat can be filled and at the discretion of the executive. Due to the clubs financial commitments to operators etc, we are unable to offer a refund due to member cancellations with less than 30 days notice (except under exceptional circumstances)
  • Refunds due to extenuating circumstances (death, serious illness) will be at the discretion of the club executive and will be decided on a case by case basis or before October 31st.
  • Any questions on this policy may be brought up to the attention of the President, Vice President, Treasurer, or the Charter Coordinator for clarification.
  • If either the Durham Divers Scuba Club or the Captain of the charter choose to cancel the charter for any reason, a full refund will be provided.
